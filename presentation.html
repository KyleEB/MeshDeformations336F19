What are Mesh Deformations?


Objects in life have the the ability to bend, twist, and warp and mesh deformations in Three JS are what allow you to do that.


What are they used for?


Deformations manipulate shapes and can be used to create life like movements. Examples can include the a simple twist, the bounce of ball, and up to more complex things like facial movements.
